Our Story

Welcome to Netro, where every cup tells a story of passion, dedication, and the finest coffee craftsmanship. Our journey began with a simple dream: to create a sanctuary where coffee lovers can experience the art of perfect brewing.

The Birth of Excellence

Founded by master baristas with decades of experience, Netro represents the pinnacle of coffee culture. We source our beans from the world's finest plantations, ensuring every sip delivers an unforgettable experience.

2020

Netro opens its first location, introducing premium coffee culture to the community.

2021

Expanded to 5 locations and won "Best Coffee Shop" award.

2023

Launched our signature roasting facility and sustainable sourcing program.

2025

Serving over 10,000 customers daily across 15 locations with the same commitment to excellence.
